Asado Style Beef Short Ribs

Description

Enjoyed by many people, this Argentinian style of grilling brings out the best flavors of the short ribs.

The taste is enhanced by the addition of the salt and lime brine.

Ingredients

  • Beef short ribs, 2inches thick
  • Salt
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Bake short ribs in 425 oven for 85min
  2. Prepare sauce by adding a pinch of salt into lime juice
  3. Baste short ribs with sauce every 20min
  4. Eat
