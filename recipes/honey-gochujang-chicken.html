Honey Gochujang Chicken

Description

First created by Taeho Lee in 2007, while attending Vassar College, Taeho created this dish as a tasty, and affordable meal.

He liked it so much, he started making it for friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken Drumsticks
  • Gochujang
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Make many perforations to the chicken drumsticks
  2. Bake in 425 oven for 45min
  3. Prepare sauce by adding 1 parts gochujang and 3 parts honey
  4. Eat
