Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A childhood classic sandwich you didn't know could get any better.

Melty, warm peanut butter and sweet jelly are perfect with a glass of cold milk.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter and jelly on either side of the bread
  2. Microwave for 30seconds
  3. Eat
